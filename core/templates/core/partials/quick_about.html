Welcome to EIP Ethiopia

Ethiopian Initiatives for Progress (EIP) is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities through sustainable development initiatives. Since our establishment in 2005, we have been working towards creating lasting positive change across Ethiopia.

Our mission is to foster sustainable development through education, healthcare, economic empowerment, and environmental conservation programs that directly benefit local communities.
