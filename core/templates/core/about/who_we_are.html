    Our History

    From Humble Beginnings

    Founded in 2005 by a group of passionate Ethiopian professionals, EIP Ethiopia began as a small community initiative in Addis Ababa. Our founders recognized the need for sustainable, community-driven development programs that address root causes rather than symptoms.

    What started as a volunteer-based organization working in two communities has grown into a nationally recognized non-profit operating in multiple regions across Ethiopia.

    Our Mission & Vision

    Our Mission

    To empower Ethiopian communities through sustainable development initiatives that promote education, healthcare, economic empowerment, and environmental conservation, fostering self-reliance and improved quality of life.

    • Promote sustainable community development
    • Foster education and capacity building
    • Improve access to healthcare services
    • Support economic empowerment initiatives

    Our Vision

    To see a prosperous Ethiopia where all communities have access to opportunities, resources, and support needed to achieve sustainable development and improved quality of life.

    • Self-reliant communities across Ethiopia
    • Equitable access to education and healthcare
    • Sustainable environmental practices
    • Strong local economies and livelihoods

    Our Core Values

    Integrity

    We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our work.

    Community First

    Communities are at the center of all our initiatives.

    Sustainability

    Accountability
